# Shory.

**AI-Powered SME Insurance Platform for Businesses**

Executive Summary — April 2026

---

## THE PROBLEM

UAE SMEs face significant barriers to insurance compliance: 2–3 day quote turnaround, 12+ field requirements, AED 1.2B in annual MOHRE fines, 70% over/under-insured due to lack of guidance, zero price transparency, and minimal post-sale policy management.

---

## THE SOLUTION

Shory is a UAE Insurance Authority licensed digital broker that reduces the insurance journey from 2–3 days to under 3 minutes through AI-powered automation, multi-insurer comparison, and comprehensive lifecycle management.

---

## CORE FEATURES

### Customer Platform

- **4 Smart Entry Paths:** Quick Select (30s), OCR Upload (45s), AI Advisor (90s), Manual Form (3min)
- **Real-time quotes** from 6 UAE insurers
- **Emirates ID verification** (UAE PASS simulation)
- **Modern payment** (Apple Pay, Google Pay, cards)
- **Customer dashboard** (policies, renewals, claims)
- **Bilingual:** English + Khaleeji Arabic

### Admin Portal

- **Platform health monitoring** (13 services)
- **AI correlation engine** (platform ↔ behavior)
- **Customer lifecycle management**
- **Churn prediction & AI playbooks**
- **Proactive intelligence** (weather, peers, gov data)
- **Real-time funnel analytics**
