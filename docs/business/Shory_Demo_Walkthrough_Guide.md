## ANTICIPATED Q&A

### Q: How do you make money?

**A:** Standard broker commissions. We earn ~17.5% of gross written premium. Year 1 target: AED 110M GWP = AED 19.25M commission revenue. EBITDA positive by Year 2 at ~49% margins.

### Q: What's your competitive advantage vs. traditional brokers?

**A:** Speed (< 3 min vs. 2-3 days), Transparency (multi-insurer comparison), Intelligence (AI at every step), and Lifecycle (complete post-sale management). We're also licensed by the UAE Insurance Authority as a digital broker.

### Q: Is the OCR real or simulated?

**A:** Real. Powered by Anthropic's Claude vision API. 12-field extraction with confidence scoring and UAE authenticity verification. 95%+ accuracy target.

### Q: What about customer acquisition cost?

**A:** Target CAC: AED 150-200 per policy. Channels: Government partnerships (TAMM integration), Digital marketing, Referral program (AED 200 off for both parties).

### Q: How do you handle claims?

**A:** We're a broker, not an underwriter. Claims go directly to the insurer. We provide the interface for filing and tracking. Our admin portal flags customers with open claims for retention attention.

### Q: What's the regulatory status?

**A:** UAE Insurance Authority digital broker license required (application in progress). Full compliance with UAE Data Protection Law, MOHRE regulations, and AML/KYC requirements.

### Q: How does bilingual support work?

**A:** Full English and Arabic. Not translation-as-afterthought — RTL layout, IBM Plex Arabic font, Khaleeji dialect for AI (not formal MSA). Cultural adaptation throughout.

### Q: What's the tech stack?

**A:** Frontend: React 18 + Tailwind CSS. AI: Anthropic Claude API (Sonnet 4 + Haiku 4.5). Maps: OpenStreetMap. Payment: FINWAL partnership (monthly installments). Government APIs: UAE PASS, TAMM (integration planned).

### Q: Why Saudi Arabia next?

**A:** Larger market (USD 2.4B vs. USD 1.2B). Similar regulatory framework (SAMA vs. UAE IA). 1.3M SMEs. Different government channels (Maroof, Fasah vs. TAMM) but same core product logic.

### Q: What about insurer relationships?

**A:** Partnership agreements with 6 UAE insurers: GIG Gulf, Orient, Liva, Sukoon, Salama, Watania Takaful. Production requires API integration (currently simulated quotes in MVP).
